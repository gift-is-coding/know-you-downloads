Know You · macOS · Local-first journaling

Turn your day on the computer into a readable story.

Know You captures your daily computer context on macOS, filters sensitive data before persistence, and turns the day into a story-first journal you can actually read instead of a pile of raw logs.

Local-first by default

Clipboard and notification context stays on your Mac, with privacy filtering applied before data is written to disk.

Story-first reading

You read the day as a narrative first, then drill down into paragraph-level source evidence when you want context.

Works even without cloud AI

Fallback story generation keeps the daily journal usable when no external summarizer is configured.

Install

  1. Download the zip file.
  2. Unzip it and move KnowYou.app into /Applications.
  3. Launch the app and complete the macOS permission flow for clipboard and notifications.
